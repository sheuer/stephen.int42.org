Stephen Lyle Heuer

Hello, I am Stephen, co-founder of Arux Software based in Downtown Minneapolis, Minnesota. I have a deep passion for sustainable living and environmental conservation, which is reflected in my personal life. Growing up in a third-tier suburb that heavily relied on cars made me think that cars were the best. But once I moved to the city and I transitioned to using bicycles and public transit for 90% of my transportation needs my eyes started to open about how cars ruin cities. One of my favorite bikes is my Larry vs Harry eBullitt cargo bike that has helped me replace so many car trips and has changed me to think first about using a bike for all trips, in all weather.

During my free time, I love exploring the outdoors through camping, hiking, canoeing, and swimming. I'm also an adventurous foodie, always eager to try new culinary experiences. At home, I've transformed my yard into a beautiful mix of Minnesota native prairie and a bountiful vegetable garden. I also designed and built a custom chicken coop and treehouse with the help of my family.

I have always been passionate about computers, starting with the Apple Macintosh 512k Enhanced that I used as a child. I built my own computers during my teenage years and learned various web technologies, including PHP, HTML, and JavaScript. In college, I took classes to learn Java, which is the only programming language that I did not teach myself. After starting Arux Software, I taught myself Ruby and other technologies to continue building innovative software solutions for our clients.
