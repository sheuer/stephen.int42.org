Stephen Lyle Heuer

Hello, my name is Stephen, and I am a co-founder of Arux Software, located in beautiful Downtown Minneapolis, Minnesota. I have a passion for sustainable living and environmental conservation, which is reflected in my personal life. As a child raised in a third-tier suburb heavily dependent on cars, I held the belief that cars represented freedom and were the optimal form of transportation. Upon relocating to the city, and shifting to biking and public transit for the majority of my transportation requirements, my perception began to shift. With the addition of children and my significant other's adoption of biking, my perspective underwent a significant transformation, and I gained a deeper comprehension of how cars ruin cities. One of my favorite bikes is my Larry vs Harry eBullitt cargo bike, which has enabled me to substitute numerous car journeys and has transformed my mindset to prioritize biking for all types of trips, regardless of the weather conditions.

During my free time, when I'm not biking, I love exploring the outdoors through camping, hiking, canoeing, and swimming. I'm also an adventurous foodie, always eager to try new culinary experiences. At home, I've transformed my yard into a beautiful mix of Minnesota native prairie and a bountiful (sometimes) vegetable garden. We also keep chickens, and I took the lead in designing and constructing a chicken coop and treehouse, with the assistance of my family.

I have always been passionate about computers, starting with the Apple Macintosh 512k Enhanced that I used as a child. I built my own computers during my teenage years and learned various web technologies, including PHP, HTML, and JavaScript. In college, I took classes to learn Java, which is the only programming language that I did not teach myself. After starting Arux Software, I taught myself Ruby and other technologies to continue building innovative software solutions for our clients.
